# Proven Talent Highlight Reel: Core Interview Questions

The interview is structured around four short video responses.

The goal is to help the candidate tell their story, explain meaningful work, show how they think, and reflect on growth.

---

## 1. Your Story

### Main Question

**Tell me about yourself and what brought you to this point in your career.**

### What This Reveals

- Motivation
- Communication style
- Career clarity
- Personal story
- Confidence

### Example Follow-Up Questions

- You mentioned a career transition. What made that move meaningful for you?
- What kind of work gives you the most energy?
- What are you hoping to grow into next?
- What experiences shaped the way you think about your career?

### Highlight Reel Theme

**Why this candidate**

---

## 2. Meaningful Work

### Main Question

**Looking at your background, tell me about one project, role, or accomplishment that best represents work you are proud of.**

### What This Reveals

- Ownership
- Relevant experience
- Impact
- Depth of work
- Decision-making

### Example Follow-Up Questions

- What was your personal contribution?
- What was the hardest part of that work?
- How did you know the work was successful?
- What tradeoff did you have to make?
- What would you do differently if you did it again?

### Highlight Reel Theme

**Relevant experience**

---

## 3. How You Think

### Main Question

**Based on your background and goals, imagine you are joining a new team where you need to make an impact in your first 90 days. Walk me through how you would learn, prioritize, and contribute.**

### What This Reveals

- Problem-solving
- Practical judgment
- Prioritization
- Role readiness
- Strategic thinking
- Career alignment

### Example Follow-Up Questions

- What would you try to learn in your first two weeks?
- How would you decide which problems are worth solving first?
- Who would you want to build relationships with early on?
- How would your previous experience help you in this situation?
- What would success look like after 90 days?

### Highlight Reel Theme

**How they solve problems**

---

## 4. Reflection

### Main Question

**Tell me about a time you struggled, failed, or received difficult feedback. What happened, and what did you learn?**

### What This Reveals

- Self-awareness
- Resilience
- Accountability
- Humility
- Growth mindset
- Culture fit

### Example Follow-Up Questions

- What did that experience change about how you work?
- How did you handle the situation in the moment?
- What would you do differently now?
- How did you move forward after that experience?
- What did you learn about yourself?

### Highlight Reel Theme

**How they handle setbacks**

---

# Candidate-Facing Framing

This is not a test.

It is a guided conversation to help you show more than what fits on a resume.

You will answer four short prompts:

1. **Your Story** — who you are and what brought you here.
2. **Meaningful Work** — work you are proud of.
3. **How You Think** — how you would approach a new challenge.
4. **Reflection** — what you have learned from struggle or feedback.

You control when recording starts.

Nothing is shared until you choose to submit.
